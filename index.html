Welcome to jsVislice

jsVislice is a JavaScript version of the pen and paper game Hangman (gallows == vislice in Slovenian) with a twist. After correctly guessing the word, you don't start over again, but advance to the next level with a harder word. How far can you get?

How to play?

The game selects a random word depending on your current level. The first and the last letter are shown and the remaining letters are representend as underscores (with the exception of those equal to the first and last letter). Your goal is to guess the correct word in limited number of tries. You will loose a life point with a wrong guess and if you loose all of them it's game over.

But fear not, mighty adventurer, when you correctly guess a word, your health bar will refill and every five levels you will gain a bonus health point. How far can you get?
